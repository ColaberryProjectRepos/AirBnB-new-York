How to create new measure

   1. Click on the right top corner New Measure Tab  or Right click on Dashboard table

   2. Select New Measure

  3. Type Measure Name ---> “=” sign ---> DAX 

4.  Click Enter

   5.  Repeat step (1) to (4) to create all Measures
